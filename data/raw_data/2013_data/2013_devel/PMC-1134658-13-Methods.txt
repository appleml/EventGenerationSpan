Cell sorting

Highly purified CD19+CD27- or CD19+CD27+ cells were obtained by staining CD19+ cells with anti-CD27 PE and CD19 PC5 mAbs for 30 minutes at 4degreesC, followed by washing with PBS and sorting on FACS DiVa from Becton Dickinson.
