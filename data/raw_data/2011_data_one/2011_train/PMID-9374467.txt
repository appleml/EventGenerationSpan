The nuclear factor of activated T cells (NFAT) group of transcription factors is retained in the cytoplasm of quiescent cells.

NFAT activation is mediated in part by induced nuclear import.

This process requires calcium-dependent dephosphorylation of NFAT caused by the phosphatase calcineurin.

The c-Jun amino-terminal kinase (JNK) phosphorylates NFAT4 on two sites.
